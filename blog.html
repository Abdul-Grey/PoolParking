How I get Parking Pool idea?

I was spending my summer vacation in Hyderabad. It wasn't even a good choice and on top of that heat was so harsh.Yes, you may think I'm crazy. But I did.
I'd to go for shopping during that summer vacation. The heat was about 50° C and I was stuck in the traffic for half an hour and had to wait for another 30-40 minutes for parking space.I was totally slammed. Then this idea struck out of nowhere. Why can't I book parking place. I know that I can't handle the traffic, but I can control the parking.
Come on, people how many of us face parking problems everyday.
I gave a thought about this and come up with Parking Pool idea. I worked my buns off to get it done. Now, here I am.

Was it easy for me to put Parking Pool into action?

It was not. I did a little research on the Internet. Interestingly, I found that there are services like I want to create. And I thought, I should give up this idea? Someone already did.One of my friends said, Everything is a remix. There are parking services similar to you. But none of them is like you and don't share your values, idea. And he showed me a video on Everything is a remix. It's amazing. And that friend is Hemanth. He and I are partners. We share similar values.
